Who I am

Hello, I’m Guido. I live in Geneva, Switzerland and I do physics at the Theoretical Physics Department of CERN. Before moving here, I lived in New York City, working at the Center for Cosmology and Particle Physics, of New York University. In 2010, I got my PhD in Astroparticle Physics at SISSA, in Trieste.

My main research interest is Theoretical Cosmology. I study the birth and evolution of the universe, from its (pre-)infancy to today. You can have a look at my publications here, and feel free to contact me at guidodamico_at_nyu.edu. Here’s my academic CV.
